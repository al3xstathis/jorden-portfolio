About Me

I'm a 22 year old student-athlete who continues to chase her dreams
Born and raised in Montreal, Quebec.
I'm also an elite track and field sprinter and long jumper.
I made the Youth National Track and field team in 2015 and am
striving to make my first Senior team.


Despite having to go through two difficult knee surgeries, I overcame adversity.
This took an enormous amount of patience, dedication and perseverance,
three qualities about myself which allow me to reach the top.

Modelling is something that brings me so much joy.

It allows me to express my true colors as a person and my love for fashion.
As an extrovert, I easily connect and work very well with different people.
My hard work and positive attitude enables me to achieve my goals!

My hobbies include: Sports, Singing, Coding, and Cooking.
